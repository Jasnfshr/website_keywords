Humble Hobbies offer instruction in
*basic sewing
*quilting
*cross stitch
*embroidery
*crochet 
*knit
*general crafting

Come sew with us!!!
*Sewing reinforces many skills we are teaching our children.  In addition to stretching their creativity, the children also practice fine motor, hand-eye coordination, math and reading skills, patience, following directions, as well as building their self confidence! Sign-up for a class!  

*We sew in positive and "no stress" environment!!!  We focus on learning and having fun!  We do not make mistakes, instead we have learning opportunities!

*The children are learning and Moms are wanting to join the fun!  Moms, come join us too!  It has been proven that hobbies help reduce stress.  It's the NEW YOGA!!!  Give it a try!


Classes are offered in my home for children, teens and adults!   
  • *Children will learn basic sewing, machine and hand sewing.
  •  Sewing classes are small to give each child the attention they need!  3-4 children max!
