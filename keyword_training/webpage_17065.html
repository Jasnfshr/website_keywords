    Gucci’s Set To Show At One Of London’s Most Iconic Landmarks

    It’s never been done before, but the grand and glorious Westminster Abbey is poised to host its very first catwalk show this summer. 

    And, as you might expect, it’s one big name brand that’s set to take to the historic landmarks cloisters – Gucci. 

    Shame it’s not a British brand, eh?

    The luxe Italian label revealed to The Telegraph that it will hold it’s next Cruise show at the abbey on June 2, marking the first time that a fashion house of any nationality has been granted permission to show there.

    Unsurprisingly, the brand’s creative director Alessandro Michele – who’s just embarked on his second year at the company – describes the news as “magical”.

    “London is always on my mind and in my memories,” he told the paper. “I’m obsessed with British culture, past and present. To be able to show the collection inside the Cloisters in Westminster Abbey is magical.”

    As well as famously hosting an array of royal events, including the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge’s wedding and Princess Diana’s funeral, the Gothic building is where the likes of Isaac Newton, Geoffrey Chaucer and Charles Darwin are buried – so it’s a pretty big deal.

    The Duchess Of Cornwall Just Rocked One Glam Red Carpet Look

    The Duchess of Cornwall looked resplendent at a royal function last night. [Photo: Rex]

    Whether we’re open about it or not, we all love perusing the Duchess of Cambridge’s style file. And taking a peek inside the Queen’s wardrobe is just as fascinating. But Camilla’s ensembles rarely get a look in. Possibly because she doesn’t tend to don outfits that are all that newsworthy. Until now.

    The Duchess of Cornwall got her glam on last night, bringing a bucket load of Hollywood chic to the red carpet. The 68-year-old dressed up to the nines, wearing the ultimate evening dress to the British Asian Trust dinner in London.

    She donned an embellished Bruce Oldfield gown for the occasion, made from a midnight blue fabric and boasting intricate beading and a full skirt. 

    Camilla paired the frock with a black shawl, which hopefully helped keep the winter chill over her skin, and accessoried with some wow-factor diamonds – like only the female members of the royal family can.

    An unlikely style hero, but one to watch perhaps. [Photo: Rex]

    Prince Charles and Duchess weren’t the only high-profile people to adorn the guest list. Some 450 guests attended the lavish event, held at the National History Museum, including the likes of Simon Cowell, Leona Lewis, Labyrinth and Neelan Gill.

    According to the Daily Mail, all of the invitees indulged in a mouth-watering Indian banquet, which was no doubt divine. 

    Here’s hoping Camilla managed to avoid dripping anything down her resplendent frock. Cleaning mango chutney out of that bodice’s beadwork would be any dry cleaner’s ideal of hell.

    Heroic Woman Takes Stand Against Catcalling: “Get Your S*** Together Guys”

    After being yelled out by a passer-by, Brandt decided to educate the world on the issue of catcalling. [Photo: Facebook/Christen Brandt]

    Being called “sexy” (or some “complimentary” equivalent) by a random pedestrian or driver is never nice. It doesn’t give a woman a confidence boost, it doesn’t make her feel special and singled out, what it does do is make her jump out of her skin and feel slightly violated.  

    It’s objectifying and something that almost every single woman has to put up with – but she shouldn’t have to. But despite how prominent an issue it is, very few women call it out. But one New Yorker has.

    Christen Brandt has gone viral after taking to Facebook to share one particular catcalling incident, which left her feeling upset and angry – despite having done absolutely nothing to provoke her harasser.

    Explaining that she was wrapped up in a parka, scarf and knee-high boots to protect herself form the winter chill, one unknown man still felt the need to openly perv on her.

    “This is what I was wearing this morning as I made my way through a crowded 34th Street subway station, and a man passing by me said, ‘Damn, you have some great legs’,” she wrote.

    “When I ignored him and kept walking, he turned around to follow me, to get closer, even as I was moving away. ‘Did you hear me, honey? I said you have nice legs. Damn! Thank you’.”

    Like most of us, Brandt has been the victim of unwanted attention from a member of the opposite sex. [Photo: Facebook/Christen Brandt]

    Brandt continued to explain that it was the demand to be thanked that really got to her. “As if my 5 inches of legging-covered skin were there for him. Given as a gift wrapped in brown tights. Existing in the world for him to appreciate, or not,” she wrote.

    “Next time you wonder whether your skirt is too short, next time you ask your teen daughter to change her clothes, or the next time you hear about school dress codes in the news, remember this photo. I am in a fucking parka and boots. And it. doesn’t. matter. All women have these moments. All of us. And yet the world acts as if it’s still our problem to fix. Get your shit together, guys. I, for one, am sick of dealing with it.”

    Clearly Brandt’s post has struck a chord with other women worldwide: Since being posted it’s been shared over 40,000 times and been liked by almost 75,000 people.

    Catherine Zeta-Jones Puts The Cosmetic Surgery Rumours To Rest Once And For All

    The Welsh actress’ good looks have fuelled rumours for years that she’s been under the knife. [Photo: Larsen & Talbert/Corbis Outline]

    Catherine Zeta-Jones is one of those women that seems to defy nature. She’s pushing 50, but with that line-free skin, clear complexion and incredibly toned and taut body, she doesn’t look a day over 35.  

    It’s precisely her good genes that have caused numerous fans to question whether the Welsh-born star has been under the knife. The answer? No. Zeta-Jones, who hit headlines last week when she rocked up to the “Dad’s Army” premiere in London showing more than a flash of flesh, really is just that extraordinarily well honed and beautiful.  

    But while the actress is quick to admit that she hasn’t felt the need to get any nipping and tucking done yet, she isn’t against it. In fact, she fully supports women who decide to get a little cosmetic surgery done – and admits she may still get a little touching up done herself.  

    “You don’t have to be a beauty queen to be an actress,” Zeta-Jones told Good Housekeeping. 

    “The roles that are coming my way are different and more interesting. But I’m not anti-plastic surgery at all. Contrary to public opinion, I have not been under the knife…yet, is what I say! If I feel like it, I’m going to go ahead and do it! If (surgery) makes you feel better, who am I to tell someone that it’s wrong?”

    Her slim frame and enviable figure have left fans wondering if she’s had a little cosmetic surgery done. [Photo: Larsen & Talbert/Corbis Outline]

    Instead, the “Dad’s Army” star sticks to a strict workout regime to keep her skin glowing and her body in shape. Even Hollywood actresses have to work hard to maintain their good looks.  

    “I have my dancing. I’m lucky that I have a bit of a muscle memory, so once my body gets back in motion it seems to remember,” she said. “Being active and on my feet is much healthier for my psyche. I really feel it’s healthy for a body to sweat every day. I run on my treadmill or power walk outside. Then I do some bodywork and arm work and a bit of tap dancing every now and then.”

    She’s previously revealed that she doesn’t have a personal trainer or dietician (although that could’ve changed now), admitting that she doesn’t “need anyone” to motivate her. If only we could all boast that mentality, eh?

    Brooklyn Beckham And Burberry Reveal Unlikely Collaboration

    The 16-year-old star has announced a new job he’s scored at Burberry. [Photo: Instagram/Brooklyn Beckham]

    Brooklyn Beckham isn’t content with being a model, social media star and the son of Victoria and David, he’s now decided to turn his hand at photography. And we’re not talking about him taking semi-serious snapshots for his fans to peruse on his Instagram account. Instead, the 16-year-old is set to shoot a campaign for Burberry – yes, Burberry.

    The eldest Beckham son took to his social media accounts today to announce the news, revealing that he’ll be capturing the images for the brand’s Brit fragrance tomorrow, during a shoot that will be broadcast live on social media. No pressure.  

    “Excited to photograph @Burberry campaign tomorrow,” he wrote on Twitter and Instagram. “Watch it live on their Instagram & Snapchat #THISISBRIT.”

    Brooklyn’s younger brother, Romeo, has been modelling for Burberry for over three years now. [Photo: Instagram/Burberry]

    It’s not the first time a member of the Beckham clan has collaborated with the iconic British label. Romeo, Victoria and David’s middle son, has been modelling for the company since 2012 and has starred in a number of the brand’s campaigns.

    This could well be the start of Brooklyn’s budding photography career, which he made clear he was interested in during an interview with Miss Vogue last year.

    “I love football, but I’m also very passionate about photography and film,” he said. “I’m keeping my options open right now.”
