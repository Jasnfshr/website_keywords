Featured Program

Machine Tool Technology

It’s an industry that can build your dreams for tomorrow into a reality today!

The Machine Tool Technology program at Georgia Northwestern Technical College gives you the skills and background you’ll need to make your career happen!

When you earn a Machine Tool Technology diploma from GNTC, you have gained a new way of life for you and your family. A Machine Tool specialist can design each piece at the heart of every machine the 21st century world can imagine!

Build your career from scratch by taking the first step today at Georgia Northwestern Technical College. At GNTC, your hands and your mind will work together to create the next big thing from the inside, out!

From programming the latest computer-controlled machinery to learning to construct each phase of a project by design, the Machine Tool Technology program will meet you where you are and take you where you want to be!

Get Focused. Get Hired!
