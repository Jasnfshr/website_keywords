More about Carfinance247

At Carfinance247 we make it our business to provide cost-effective loans for anyone looking to purchase a new or a used car, no matter what their financial circumstances. We pride ourselves on delivering the very best customer service, market-leading rates and can also provide van and motorbike finance!

Our service provides an easy, hassle-free way for you to apply for a car loan and get the vehicle of your choice from any reputable dealer nationwide. Benefits include:

  • Exclusive low rates (from 5.8% APR)
  • No deposit required
  • All credit circumstances considered
  • A decision within minutes
  • Your very own personal finance advisor

All you need to do is complete our straightforward application to receive an almost instant decision. If you are accepted, our experienced advisors will be on hand to discuss your every requirement.

About our car finance deals

At Carfinance247 our aim is to provide car loans that cost you less! We will get you the most competitive rates relative to your personal circumstances from our large panel of specialist lenders.

We can provide finance for all employment types from company directors to self-employed taxi drivers. We can also help you to obtain a car loan if you currently have bad credit or have been refused in the past. Typical examples of credit issues include, but are not limited to, CCJ's, arrears, defaults and missed payments. People in debt management plans and IVA's needn’t worry either.

Of course, if we feel that a car loan is not in your best financial interests, then we will let you know. We are a responsible business and while we can help most people, we cannot guarantee the offer of car finance.
