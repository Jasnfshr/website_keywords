Oath of the Gatewatch Prerelease at Showcase Swarthmore

Posted by ShowcaseComicsandGames on January 8, 2016 at 5:45 PM comments (0)

Showcase Comics in Swarthmore will be holding our Oath of the Gatewatch prereleases Saturday and Sunday January 16th and 17th.  On Saturday January 16 our first prerelease will have registration start at 11:00 am and start handing out packs at 12:00 pm.  Later that day we will be hosting a Two Headed Giant event with registration starting at 5:00 pm and packs being handed out at 6:00 pm.  Teams must register together.

And on Sunday January 17th registration will begin at 11:00 am and packs will be handed out at 12:00 pm

Updated Auction List

Posted by ShowcaseComicsandGames on November 4, 2015 at 4:25 PM comments (0)

It's Auction Time!

Here's the updated list for our Auction coming up on November 8th at the Swarthmore PA store. The store opens at 11am and you can view the items for sale. I start yelling and screaming noon, as we go through all the large items. Towards the end of the day, we'll do "buy it now" rounds. Everything starts at 25.00 a lot, and works it's way down until it's a dollar a lot.

We have a huge amount of stuff for this one. Warmachine and Warhammer armies, figure cases, Forgeworld stuff, boxes of random bitz, and tubs of scenery. We have 550 lots so far, and I haven't added the scenery and items the store will be putting in yet. Time to come bid and pick up some bargains.

For info give Mike a call at the Showcase Comics in Swarthmore, 610-544-9229, or email me at ShowcasePA@aol.com

This is a live auction, sorry but we can't take bids ahead of time.
