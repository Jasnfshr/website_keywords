A monthly column by Marilyn Gamm

Read the February edition of Great Co-Mission by Marilyn Gamm, chair of the Presbyterian Mission Agency Board, in which she calls Presbyterians to co-missionally partner with God to advocate for another by addressing the suppression of political and economic rights of people of color, immigrants, workers, and the poor in the U.S. and abroad.
