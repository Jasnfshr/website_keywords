In a Nutshell Miniatures


Located in historic downtown Mt. Dora, FL, In a Nutshell Miniatures is a full-line retail dollhouse and miniature store.

Dollhouses


In a Nutshell Miniatures carries a wide variety of dollhouses, including kits. Custom construction or remodeling is also available.

Accessories


From Reutter Porcelain and flooring to furniture and tools, In a Nutshell Miniatures has all of your dollhouse and miniature accessories.

Collectibles


We additionally carry a wide variety of collectibles including Boyds Bears Jim Shore pieces, such as his Disney, Wizard of Oz, and Angel lines.

Additional Collectibles


A wide range of collectibles are also available, including Christmas and other holiday pieces.

Tools


In a Nutshell Miniatures carries all the tools for your miniatures and model collections including paint, brushes, glue, and more.

Models


We carry a number of models including cars, ships, and planes from a variety of manufacturers.

Dollhouses and Miniatures in Mount Dora, FL

Welcome to In A Nutshell Miniatures, a full-line retail dollhouse and miniature store located in historic downtown in Mt. Dora, FL.

We carry a variety of miniature items, including a lumberyard, electrical supplies, wallpaper, flooring, hardware, landscaping, furniture, kits and accessories.
