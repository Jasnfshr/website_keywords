US Services Sector Growth At 2-Year Low

The ISM Non-Manufacturing PMI index declined to 53.5 in January of 2016, compared to 55.8 in December and below market forecasts. It is the lowest reading since February of 2014 as activity, new orders, employment and prices all decreased.
