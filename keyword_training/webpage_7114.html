GRIMBETS is an analytical center of sports forecast designed for helping players in the bookmaker office and betting exchanges to make money on the basis of services for different sports.
GRIMBETS includes more than 15 different services based on the best modified and improved systems and strategies for sports forecast.
GRIMBETS includes only the most popular sports such as football, tennis, basketball, hockey, volleyball and horse racing.
GRIMBETS monitors changes of the line, funds distribution and team’s form in the match. It compares the line, season, team series and statistics. It analyses and forecasts the total, outcome and other different types of bets
GRIMBETS collects the online information about world events in the sport, displays complete statistics and forecasts according to what happens during the sporting event.
GRIMBETS is equipped with a very easy navigation, recognized as the most effective by many players. This navigation makes it possible to remove or mark events of interest in the sport world, obtain information in the most convenient and operational form to carry out a quality analysis for making a decision when choosing a bet.
Individual forecasting systems, different helps and effective methods for their implementation are built into GRIMBETS. It will be easy for you to find description, help and method of application for each value, selection criteria, button and every element.
GRIMBETS is equipped with Bot services for Betfair exchange, adjusted individually for player’s needs. With the help of these services a player can completely or partially give account to robot, which will analyze and place bets in the system chosen by the player.
Each service in GRIMBETS has a statistical archive, which allows you to check the data for each event in the past to see and understand how the service works and how you can use it more effectively
