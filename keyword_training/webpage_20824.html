Welcome to Pets Keepers Guide ! – A Pet Forum Community to Discuss all Pet Species

Before the humans even had a written history tens thousands of years ago, our ancestors had already started to live with some animal species not meant for food. Such scenes could be seen on some cave paintings. These are the animals we call pets. A pet is a companion animal we love and care for. Some of them are also capable of love and care for us. Today, there are hundreds millions of people around the world have one or more pets at home. In the United States alone, over 73 million families or about 60% of all U.S. households have one or more pets according to National Pet Owners Survey. We the pet owners are one big family who share the same interest.

An online forum is a place where people with same interests but from different places can join together to discuss the same hobbies and topics. If you are a pet lover and love to talk about it, then you have come to the right place! Pets Keepers Guide is an online community dedicated to discuss ALL pet species. We have dog forum, cat forum, fish forum, bird forum, reptile forum, insect and arthropod forum, etc. everything is included! Join the free community now! It will only take a minute to sign up! Discuss anything pet related with other people from all over the world! Introduce yourself and your animal companion(s), ask questions, and help others, share experience, knowledge, stories, show off photos and videos. If you are an experienced pet owner and also a good writer, we are seeking more articles for our new but expanding library section. Please contact us if you are interested in having your own articles published online! Let the fun begin! Join now to be a part of the community today! It is FREE!
